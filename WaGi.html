Short description

      We are Against Ignorence is a "homemade" website designed to help people learn new and exciting things in a fun way. It is entirely on the web with no need to download anything. Of course that means you need to have access to the internet.It's 2018 let's be real you can easily get wifi/ethernet access. You can share your knowledge. What do we mean by that ? By playing our game you help the community of StackExchange grow , either by upvoting already accepted answer or by providing your own anwer.
