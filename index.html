What Is WAGI ?

We are AGainst Ignorence is a "homemade" website designed to help people learn new and exciting things in a fun way.

It is entirely on the web with no need to download anything. Of course that means you need to have access to the internet.It's 2018 let's be real you can easily get wifi/ethernet access.

You can share your knowledge. What do we mean by that ?

By playing our game you help the community of StackExchange grow , either by upvoting already accepted answer or by providing your own anwer.

It is open source. What that means ?

Well let's say it is free ! Even better , the source code is public on Github.

Why Use It ?

We designed this tool to help people who often search for an answer when they are having trouble. By making this project we hope that we can make the community of "answer seekers" a better place.

You remember that time when you had that nasty error and no one had an answer on StackOverflow ?

Well that's why we made it , to help people answer more questions , decide what answer is good or bad in order to help even more people when they need an answer.

We made it simple to use and intuitive. Anyone can use it anywhere. With the code being open source you can make a topic specific game.

How You Can Use It ?

In order to use our tool you need to make a account and log in. We dont intend to use the data like Google we just want to know who sends the answers or who upvotes/downvotes.

After you are authenticated you can go to the play page and play the game. Anyone can play so be nice and play fair!

From there you can see what questions you answered , relevant information about the answers and many other things.

Dont be afraid to use our website on mobile. We designed it so you can use it on any device !
